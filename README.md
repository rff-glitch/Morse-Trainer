# Morse Trainer

![Morse Trainer Logo](https://files.manuscdn.com/user_upload_by_module/session_file/310519663111796238/qJRWKjkHPXNmyRUb.svg)

## Unlock the Secrets of Morse Code!

Dive into the fascinating world of Morse code with the **Morse Trainer**, your ultimate companion for mastering this timeless communication method. Whether you're a curious beginner or looking to sharpen your skills, our interactive web application makes learning Morse code an enjoyable and rewarding experience. Get ready to decode signals, encode messages, and become a true Morse maestro!

## What Makes it Awesome?

*   **Dynamic Training Modes**: Challenge yourself with individual letters, expand your vocabulary with common words, or tackle complex messages with full sentences. The choice is yours!
*   **Two-Way Mastery**: Practice both listening and typing! Decode incoming Morse signals into letters, or put your knowledge to the test by encoding letters into their Morse equivalents.
*   **Personalized Pace**: Take control of your learning journey with an adjustable speed setting. Slow it down to grasp the basics, or crank it up for a real challenge as you progress.
*   **Instant Feedback**: No more guessing games! Receive immediate visual and auditory cues to celebrate your correct answers and learn from your mistakes.
*   **Track Your Triumph**: Watch your skills grow with real-time statistics on your score, winning streak, and overall accuracy. Celebrate every milestone on your path to fluency.
*   **Handy Reference Guide**: Forgot a code? No problem! A built-in Morse code reference grid is always at your fingertips for quick lookups.
*   **Sleek & Intuitive Design**: Enjoy a clean, modern interface that keeps you focused on learning, without any distractions.

## Ready to Start?

Getting started is as easy as opening the `index.html` file in your favorite web browser. Here's your quick guide to becoming a Morse code pro:

1.  **Pick Your Challenge**: Navigate through the 'Letters', 'Words', or 'Sentences' tabs to select your preferred training mode.
2.  **Set Your Speed**: Use the slider to fine-tune the Morse code playback speed. Find what works best for you!
3.  **Engage & Conquer**: The app will present you with a challenge:
    *   **Decode the Signal**: Listen carefully to the Morse code and type the corresponding letter(s) into the input field. It's like being a secret agent!
    *   **Encode the Letter**: A letter will appear, and it's your turn to translate it into Morse code. Use the `.` (dot) and `-` (dash) buttons, or for a faster experience, press `S` for a dot and `L` for a dash on your keyboard.
4.  **Check Your Work**: Hit 'Enter' or click the 'CHECK' button to see how you did.
5.  **Monitor Your Progress**: Keep an eye on your score, streak, and accuracy to see your mastery grow.
6.  **Quick Reference**: Need a reminder? Click "Morse Code Reference" to reveal a comprehensive grid of all Morse code characters.
